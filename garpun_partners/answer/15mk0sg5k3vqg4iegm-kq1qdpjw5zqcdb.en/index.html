How to connect to the program

Oct 9, 2019

Important! The participation of a client account under GCS begins only from the moment of CORRECT LINK to MCC Garpun. BOTH conditions must be followed - binding by first link and admin rights.

1. Partner account must be under Garpun Tools 613-754-8619

  • If you do not already have an account, create a ticket in partners.garpun.com and technical support staff will be able to create it for you.
  • If you already have your own account, you need to establish a connection with MCC we request access to it.
  • It is necessary to add Garpun Tools 613-754-8619 as the general manager.
    • In the Google Ads section, click on the “Connect to the program” button.
    • Select “Add my account.
    • In the window that opens, select the existing access to Google Ads or add new access.
    • In the window that opens, select one or more accounts and click“ Send Invitation. ”An application will be sent by email .
    • Follow the instructions received by email. mail
  • After the accounts are linked to MCC Garpun Tools, you need to make sure that:
    • Garpun Tools manager is the first link (the date of our manager’s binding is at least a day ahead of the other managers’s binding date)
    • Garpun Tools manager has admin rights If Garpun Tools manager is not the first link, you need to block access to other managers and then, if necessary, add them so that the binding date of our manager is at least a day earlier than the binding date of other managers. For example, if you made a binding to our manager today, then you need to add other managers tomorrow, or even better the day after tomorrow (so that 24 hours pass in all time zones). Until this condition is met, the account will not be able to participate in the Affiliate Program.
